To craft a Brewing Stand in Minecraft, place a Blaze Rod in the center slot and three Cobblestone (or Black Stone/Cobbled Deepslate in Bedrock) across the bottom row of a crafting table, which creates the stand used for making potions with water bottles, blaze powder fuel, and various ingredients.
Crafting Recipe 

• Materials: 1 Blaze Rod + 3 Cobblestone (or variants). 
• Placement: 

	• Bottom Row: Cobblestone, Cobblestone, Cobblestone. 
	• Center Slot: Blaze Rod.

How to Use a Brewing Stand (for Potions) 

1. Fuel it: Place Blaze Powder in the top-left slot to power it. 
2. Add Water Bottles: Place three Glass Bottles filled with water in the bottom three slots. 
3. Add Ingredient: Place an ingredient (like Nether Wart for an Awkward Potion) in the top slot. 
4. Brew: The potion will brew in the bottom slots, transforming from a water bottle to an awkward potion, and then to other potions with further ingredients.  

Key Ingredients & Uses 

• Blaze Powder: Fuels the stand (crafted from Blaze Rods). 
• Nether Wart: The essential first ingredient to make Awkward Potions, the base for most potions. 
• Water Bottles: Start with glass bottles filled from a water source. 
• Modifiers: Ingredients like Glowstone Dust, Redstone, or Fermented Spider Eye change potion effects.
