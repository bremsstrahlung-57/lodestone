Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world RPG set in a dystopian future driven by technology and corporate power. The game focuses on identity, mortality, and control, told through the perspective of V, a mercenary navigating Night City.

Combat supports multiple playstyles, including gunplay, hacking, and stealth. RPG systems allow deep customization, though balance varies. The city itself is dense, vertical, and visually striking.

Narrative strength lies in character interactions, especially relationships that evolve over time. Side quests often explore personal stories rather than filler content.

Despite a troubled launch, Cyberpunk 2077 succeeds in world-building and thematic ambition. It presents a flawed but compelling vision of the future, emphasizing atmosphere and narrative over mechanical perfection.
